Salala chamber Commerce site

Salala Chamber of Commerce: This name was selected because it represents the local community, highlighting its mission to support economic growth, business networking, and community collaboration.

Site Purpose

The site aims to:

  • Promote local businesses through networking events, resources, and marketing opportunities.
  • Serve as a comprehensive resource for community members, visitors, and businesses looking to invest in the region.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events are available for networking and professional development?
  • How can I become a member of the chamber, and what are the benefits?
  • What are the economic and demographic trends in Smithfield?

Color Schema

Primary Blue (#004488)

Used for headings, navigation bars, and accents.

Warm Orange (#F4A261)

Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

  • Header Font: "Lora", serif — used for headings to convey professionalism and elegance.
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif — used for paragraphs and body text to enhance readability.
